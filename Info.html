About Myself

Mark Anthony A. Reyes


I am an artist living in Naga City, currently pursuing my passion for digital animation at BISCAST.
My artistry is characterized by a unique style that blends elements of traditional art with cutting-edge digital techniques.
As a digital animation student, I am constantly honing my craft through hands-on experience with various software
programs, including 3D modeling and motion graphics. I am also skilled in graphic design, illustration, and other for
visual art, making me a versatile and adaptable creator.
I draw inspiration from a variety of sources, ranging from classic cartoons and anime to contemporary video games and pop culture.
My art reflects a deep appreciation for storytelling, as I use my skills to bring characters and worlds to life with rich detail and vivid color.
